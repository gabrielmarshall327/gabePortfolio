About the Minnesota Vikings

Team Overview

The Minnesota Vikings are a professional football team based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. They are a member of the National Football League's (NFL) NFC North division.

Team History

The Vikings were founded in 1961 and have a rich history in the NFL. They have won numerous division championships and have made several playoff appearances. The team's iconic purple and gold colors are well-known to football fans.

Current Season

The team is working hard to have a successful season and bring home another Super Bowl championship.
